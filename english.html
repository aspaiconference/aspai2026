Sessions

Session Highlights

01
Seoul Celonis Wil van der Aalst · Yonghan Lee

From Automated Chaos to Intelligent Processes: Making Enterprise AI Pay Off

Enterprises are racing to adopt AI and automation, yet automating broken processes only scales up the chaos — and too many AI initiatives fail to deliver real returns. Wil van der Aalst, the founder of process mining, joins Yonghan Lee of Celonis Korea to show how process intelligence turns automated chaos into intelligent processes — drawing on object-centric process mining and real-world Process Innovation (PI) case studies to make enterprise AI truly pay off.

02
Pohang Celonis Wil van der Aalst

Using Object-Centric Process Mining to Unravel Complexity and Enable Enterprise-Level AI/ML

Traditional process mining is tied to a single-case perspective and cannot fully capture the complex interactions of real businesses. Object-centric process mining (OCPM) models the relationships between multiple objects and events as they truly are — untangling complexity and opening a new path to enterprise-wide AI/ML.

03
Seoul · Pohang SAP Signavio Gia-Thi Nguyen · Julian Lebherz · Tony (RaeSung) Park

Process Atoms and Company Memory Turning scattered process knowledge into a company asset

This session introduces an approach that breaks processes down into their smallest units — “atoms” — and accumulates and reuses them as an organization's “company memory.” It shows how scattered process knowledge can be turned into an asset that forms the foundation for continuous improvement and automation.

04
Seoul · Pohang PuzzleData Youngill Kim, CEO

Process Excellence in the AI Era and Use Cases of a Process Intelligence Platform

This talk examines what process excellence means in the age of AI and shares real-world applications across industries using ProDiscovery, a Korea-built process mining platform. Attendees will see concrete results achieved by diagnosing and improving processes with data.

05
Seoul Samsung Jinyoun Lee

Applying Process Mining in Semiconductor Fab Operations: A Case Study

A case study of applying process mining to semiconductor fab operations — among the most complex environments in the world. It shares hands-on experience uncovering bottlenecks and anomalous flows in massive manufacturing event logs and improving process efficiency.

06
Pohang LG Bongsu Cho

Process Improvement with Vision AI

This session introduces an approach that combines Vision AI with process analysis to observe and improve flows on the ground, covering cases that connect insights from video and image data to tangible process improvements.
