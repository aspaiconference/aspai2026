Summer School Lecturers

The APPI Summer School brings together distinguished academics and industry experts from around the world to deliver a comprehensive curriculum on process mining and AI.

Lecturers

Prof. Wil van der Aalst

RWTH Aachen University, Germany

Title: Introduction to Process Mining · Process Discovery · Conformance Checking

Known as the "Godfather of Process Mining," Prof. van der Aalst is a pioneer in the field of process mining and business process management. He is the author of the seminal book "Process Mining: Data Science in Action."

Prof. Moe Wynn

Queensland University of Technology, Australia

Title: Process Data and Quality Management

Prof. Wynn conducts fundamental and applied research in data quality, process automation, and process mining.

Poornima Dhall

Celonis

Title: Celonis Hands-On

Poornima Dhall is a Lead Partner Manager at Celonis, working with organizations across the Asia-Pacific region to deploy process mining and drive process improvement in practice.

Julian Lebherz

SAP Signavio

Title: SAP Signavio Hands-On

Julian Lebherz is a strategic advisor at SAP, helping enterprises apply process intelligence and the SAP Signavio suite to understand and optimize their business processes.

Prof. Marco Comuzzi

UNIST, South Korea

Title: Predictive Process Monitoring and AI in Process Mining

Prof. Comuzzi has a long-standing academic career between Asia and Europe focusing on applications of AI in process mining.

Prof. Bernardo Nugroho Yahya

Hankook Foreign University, South Korea

Title: Applications Part 1: Federated Process Mining in Supply Chains

Prof. Yahya's research spans process mining, data mining, and industrial engineering, with a focus on applying process intelligence to supply chains and manufacturing.

Prof. Angelina Prima Kurniati

Telkom University, Indonesia

Title: Applications Part 2: Process Mining in Healthcare

Prof. Kurniati focuses on process mining in healthcare, working on data quality and the analysis of clinical and care pathways to improve health service delivery.

What You'll Learn

  • Foundational concepts and algorithms in process mining
  • Practical skills using industry-standard tools (PM4Py, Celonis)
  • Advanced topics including OCPM and predictive monitoring
  • Real-world case studies and industry applications
  • Networking opportunities with experts and peers
