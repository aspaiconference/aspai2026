Process Mining Industry Day

Date: Monday, August 24th, 2026

Venue: Seoul, Republic of Korea

The Process Mining Industry Day brings together industry practitioners, technology leaders, and researchers to explore the latest trends and applications of process mining in real-world business environments.

This event serves as a bridge between academic research and industrial practice, showcasing successful process mining implementations and discussing future directions for enterprise process intelligence.
